St. Thomas Institute For Science And Technology, Trivandrum
Presents

STIST proudly presents YASYA '25,the 11th edition of its National-level Cultural Fest.
Building on the remarkable success of its inaugural event and with a vision to provide free education,
this event is spread across various technology domains, breaking down financial barriers and empowering participants.
YASYA '25 features free workshops, dynamic competitions including a Hackathon,
with cash prizes as rewards and a platform for gamers. We aim to prepare attendees to
up-skill themselves and thrive in a collaborative environment for an innovative future.

STIST proudly presents YUKTHI '25, the 2nd edition of its National-level Tech Fest.
Built on the remarkable success of its inaugural event and with a vision to provide free education, this event
is spread across various technology domains, breaking down financial barriers and empowering participants.
YUKTHI '25 features free workshops, dynamic competitions including a 24-hour Hackathon,
with cash prizes as rewards and a platform for gamers. We aim to prepare attendees to up-skill themselves and thrive in a collaborative environment for an innovative future.
