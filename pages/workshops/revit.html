REVIT WORKSHOP

Ready to build the future? Dive into Building Information Modeling (BIM) with Revit Architecture at YUKTHI'25! Learn to create stunning 3D models and revolutionize civil engineering. In this hands-on workshop, we’ll guide you through the exciting world of BIM and teach you how to design and create architectural models using Revit. Whether you're a beginner or have some interest in design, this workshop will give you the tools to explore the world of BIM!
